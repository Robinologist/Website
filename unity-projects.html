Although math isn't my best subject, I really enjoy programming. In 2023, I started teaching myself the Unity game engine to explore making games, and since then I have amassed a wealth of small coding projects and game prototypes while learning new techniques.

Here are some examples of those exploratory projects and what they've taught me:

– Rhythm Game Brawler –

While working on this first project, I learned about finite state machines and about how rhythm games synchronize events along the musical measurements of beats and bars. I also greatly improved my pixel art and animation skills throughout the project's development.

– Centipede –

In my second project, I learned and implemented a procedural animation technique called inverse kinematics to simulate the leg movements of 2D centipede and spider sillhouettes.
